harm.reduction

Drug use is directly and indirectly responsible for 11.8 million deaths each year. Over 350,000 die from overdoses (alcohol and illicit drug use disorders) each year.

We are an upcoming platform where substance users are able to make safe decisions about using different drugs, for medicinal or recreational purposes.

We would like to build a community where people can use information from scientific literature and community sourced experiences to be able to have safe experiences.
